Project Alexandria

Preserving Literature for Generations

Welcome to Project Alexandria, a project safeguarding literature and historically influential manuscripts for future generations. Inscribing these literary treasures on individual Satoshis, the smallest unit of Bitcoin, leveraging the most robust decentralized network.

As time progresses, Project Alexandria will grow and evolve into a vast repository of knowledge and cultural heritage, ensuring the continuity of human wisdom and artistic brilliance for generations to come.

The content stored on Project Alexandria is sourced from public domain repositories or acquired with explicit permissions from copyright holders. Our dedication to respecting copyright and intellectual property rights remains unwavering.

We invite you to join us in this noble quest to safeguard knowledge and preserve the legacy of human creativity. By supporting Project Alexandria, you play a pivotal role in securing the continuity of human thought and expression.
